Midwest Regionals

I was representing Illinois at the Midwest Regionals swim meet and was scared becuase this was a make it or break it meet. That kind of preasure made me question myself as a swimmer and didn't know if my times would be good enough. On the drive to the University I thought what if I got worse rather than better and that thought scared me.

Starting High School

After spending months trying to find what high school I was going to I ended up going to GCE Lab School. After that the forst day of orientation came along and my nerves we're pretty high. Not only did I come to this school knowing no one, I was also a freshmen meaning I was "fresh meat". It felt as if I was back in kindergarten holding on to my mom asking her not to let me go and to take me back home. I was suprised to actually like the first day and ended up making a friend that has been like a sister to me ever since.

Asking for Help

My parents have been fighting a lot at home and it's been stressing me and my brother out. For a while I just kept it to myself, but it felt like I was bottling up my feelings about it. It was really hard to talk about with people, but I knew reaching out for help was better than ignoring my feelings.
